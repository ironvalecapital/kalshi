# Ironvale Capital Raise Playbook

## Stage 1: Silent Proof (0-12 months)
- Build track record first.
- Minimum gates before pitching:
  - 500+ trades
  - Sharpe > 1.5
  - max drawdown < 25%
  - Monte Carlo ruin probability < 10%
  - stable calibration

## Stage 2: Soft Circle Raise (12-24 months)
- Raise small and controlled (`$25k-$100k`).
- Prioritize process transparency over return marketing.

## Stage 3: Structured Raise (2-4 years)
- Raise `250k-1M` only after multi-regime stability.
- Required packet:
  - whitepaper
  - stress tests
  - regime-attribution
  - infra and risk controls

## Stage 4: Institutional Conversations (4+ years)
- Shift to lower volatility posture.
- Tighten Kelly and concentration caps.
- Market liquidity depth becomes primary scaling constraint.

## Daily Operator Rhythm
- Pre-session risk and regime checks.
- Live session rule-based execution only.
- Post-session attribution and calibration update.
- Weekly strategy audit, monthly model/risk audit.
